The West Marches Companion App

Live Site Github Repo

This is an app meant to act as a companion to a particular style of dungeons and dragons campaign.

The noticeboard feature of the app allows players to decide what sort of adventure they want to have and who will participate in the game, taking some of the burden off the Dungeon Master.

I feel like this app has a long way to go before it's actually a viable game tool, but I enjoyed the challenge of getting all the data to work together to build these lists, and this was also my first attempt at a kind of 'access control' system for an app.

Technologies: CSS, Javascript, React, Node, Express, and PostgreSQL.
